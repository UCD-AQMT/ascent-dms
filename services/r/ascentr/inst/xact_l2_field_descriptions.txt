site_number: ASCENT site number
site_code: ASCENT site code
sample_datetime_UTC: Datetime of sample start (UTC)
sample_type: 'sample'= an ambient sample, 'QC'= a quality control measurement
element: The measured element
concentration_ng_m3: Element concentration at ambient conditions (ng/m3)
uncertainty_ng_m3: Instrument concentration uncertainty calculated based on the most updated recommendation from manufacturer at ambient conditions (ng/m3)
sample_time_min: Instrument setting for sampling duration (min)
sample_analysis_id: An id that uniquely identifies this measurement in the respective table of the ASCENT database
site_record_id: An id that identifies this measurement in the respective table of the local site database
stp_factor: Multiplication conversion factor to standard temperature (0 degrees C) and pressure (1 atm)
concentration_stp_ng_m3: Element concentration at standard temperature and pressure (ng/m3)
uncertainty_stp_ng_m3: Instrumental concentration uncertainty calculated based on the most updated recommendation from manufacturer at standard temperature and pressure (ng/m3)
flag: Data quality flag(s) as defined by EBAS (ebas.nilu.no)
qc_outcome: An overall quality control outcome for this record (1-Good, 2-Not evaluated/Unknown, 3-Questionable, 4-Bad/Invalid, 9-Missing)
comment: Comments that provide more detail about any flags applied to the record
sample_time_est_min: Estimate of actual sampling time based on measured volume and flow rate (min)
