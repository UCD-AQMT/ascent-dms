site_number: ASCENT site number
site_code: ASCENT site code
sample_datetime_UTC: Datetime of sample start (UTC)
status: Overall instrument status code (sum of all decimal values of the status flags)
cont_status: Controller status (0 – ready for operation; 100 – reset detected, not ready for operation; 255 – busy)
detect_status: Detector status
led_status: LED status (0 – Led driver initialization ERROR; 10 – Led driver OK)
valve_status: (0 - measurement; 10 - flowmeter calibration; 1100 - warm-up/clean air; 1011 - bypass)
cont_temp_degC: Control temperature (degrees C)
supply_temp_degC: Power supply board temperature (degrees C)
pressure_Pa: Reference pressure used to convert to volumetric flow (Pa)
temperature_degC: Reference temperature used to convert to volumetric flow (degrees C)
flow1_L_min: Measured flow through spot 1 (L/min)
flow2_L_min: Measured flow through spot 2 (L/min)
flowc_L_min: Common (total) flow through the optical chamber (L/min)
tape_adv_count: Number of tape advances since start
ref_[n]: Raw signal reference for channel [n]
sens1_[n]: Raw signal at sensing spot 1 for channel [n]
sens2_[n]: Raw signal at sensing spot 2 for channel [n]
bc_[n]_STP_ng_m3: Calculated BC from measurements for channel [n] at standard temperature and pressure (ng/m3)
bc1_[n]_STP_ng_m3: Uncompensated BC calculated from spot 1 for channel [n] at standard temperature and pressure (ng/m3)
bc2_[n]_STP_ng_m3: Uncompensated BC calculated from spot 2 for channel [n] at standard temperature and pressure (ng/m3)
k_[n]: Compensation parameter for channel [n]
bb_percent: Percentage of BC created by biomass burning, determined by Sandradewi model
att1_[n]: Attenuation at sensing spot 1 for channel [n]
att2_[n]: Attenuation at sensing spot 2 for channel [n]
sample_analysis_id: An id that uniquely identifies this measurement in the respective table of the ASCENT database. Currently empty for AE33 data.
site_record_id: An id that identifies this measurement in the respective table of the local site database. Currently empty for AE33 data.
qc_outcome: An overall quality control outcome for this record (1-Good, 2-Not evaluated/Unknown, 3-Questionable, 4-Bad/Invalid, 9-Missing)
flag: Data quality flag(s) as defined by EBAS (ebas.nilu.no)
comment: Comments that provide more detail about any flags applied to the record
